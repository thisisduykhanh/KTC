Meal of the Week

Herb Roasted Chicken

Ingredients:

  • boneless, skinless chicken breast(organic preferred)
  • 1 tsp Herbes de Provence
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper

Instructions:

Combine all ingredient in a plastic bag and marinate for at least an hour. Preheat oven to 350 degree. Bake chicken for 30-35 minutes or unitl juices run clear. Broil on high for two minutes or until brown. Serve with a fresh side salad.
